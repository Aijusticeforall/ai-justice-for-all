AI Justice For All is a global initiative to build a morally intelligent AI engine that detects injustice, bias, and manipulation — and proposes ethically sound verdicts.

  • ✅ Human intent & context simulation
  • ✅ Truth-checking & ethical logic verification
  • ✅ A future-proof, incorruptible justice engine

The Phase 2 AI Simulator allows us to run complex legal, moral, and ethical cases through a transparent AI engine that factors in human intent, context, impact, and proportionality.

It is designed to simulate AI-based verdicts using ethical reasoning trees, making the justice process auditable and bias-checked.
