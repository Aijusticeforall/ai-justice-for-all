AI Justice for All

“We don’t replace justice. We purify it.”

AI Justice For All is a global initiative to build a morally intelligent AI engine that detects injustice, bias, and manipulation — and proposes ethically sound verdicts. We aim to evolve justice, not replace it.

  • ✅ Human intent & context simulation
  • ✅ Truth-checking & ethical logic verification
  • ✅ A future-proof, incorruptible justice engine

🔗 View full document on Notion

The Phase 2 Simulator is the prototype of a morally intelligent system. It allows users to input real or hypothetical cases and get AI-generated, bias-checked verdicts based on intent, harm, and justice ethics.

Includes features like truth-layer logic trees, context weighting, and simulated moral perspectives.

Our timeline for Phase 2–4 will include simulator testing, ethical review collaboration, and progressive deployment to partner courts.
