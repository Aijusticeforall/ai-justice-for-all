The AI Justice Constitution defines our core values and principles. All contributors agree to this framework before participating.

Core Principles

  • Intent matters as much as action
  • Bias, manipulation, or corruption must be detectable and reversible
  • AI must always remain accountable to human dignity and life
  • Transparency in all decisions, logic, and outcomes

Contributor Agreement

By joining the AI Justice For All project, you agree to:

  • Uphold the core values of fairness, truth, and transparency
  • Contribute only ethically sound models, logic, or suggestions
  • Respect diverse human experiences and legal traditions
