The AI Justice Simulator is a testing ground for morally intelligent decision-making. In Phase 2, we begin simulating real-life cases to evaluate the ethics, reasoning, and fairness of AI verdicts. Your participation is crucial in developing a justice model that values truth over technicality.

Goals of the Simulator

  • Test AI logic on real-world legal and moral dilemmas
  • Evaluate verdicts based on human dignity and context
  • Ensure reasoning is auditable, fair, and bias-free
  • Collect public feedback to improve the justice engine

How to Contribute

  • Suggest real or hypothetical justice scenarios
  • Review AI verdicts and logic explanations
  • Help design the ethics scoring and audit mechanisms
