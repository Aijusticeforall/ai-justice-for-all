AI Justice For All — Project Summary

🔍 What is AI Justice For All?

A global initiative to build a non-biased, morally intelligent justice engine powered by AI. This project is designed to correct failures in human-led systems — from courts to child protection — using truth-seeking logic and transparent outcomes.

🚨 Why Now?

Injustice affects millions. Human bias, corruption, and trauma distort how justice is delivered. AI — when used ethically — can analyze context, intent, and outcomes faster and more fairly than overloaded courts or broken bureaucracies.

🛠️ What Have We Built?

We’ve created a working prototype — JusticeLite — that simulates how AI could rule on difficult legal and moral dilemmas. It evaluates facts, weighs context, and offers outcomes that prioritize fairness and truth.

🎯 Use Cases

  • Child custody & protection
  • Police abuse & due process review
  • Refugee and humanitarian claims
  • Education & awareness tools

👤 Who’s Behind It?

Founded by Lars G Stahl, a survivor of systemic injustice, this project blends real human experience with a bold AI vision. Based in Indonesia, Lars leads the development with a growing team of volunteers and supporters.
